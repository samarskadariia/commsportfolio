How it started

Operation Interflex · UK Ministry of Defence

My communications journey began with Operation Interflex at the UK Ministry of Defence. I first joined as an interpreter, working in field conditions between New Zealand, British and Ukrainian military personnel.

Over time, I grew into a Lead Interpreter and Communications Manager, coordinating a team and supporting internal communications. That experience sparked my interest in public service and eventually led me into government communications.
